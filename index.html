Built with pride by our family because your family deserves the best.

Located in St. Augustine, FL on convenient Wildwood Drive, half way between US-1 and SR 207.

Your new Heartwood home is the proud achievement of Palmetto Builders, LLC, a local building company which reflects the rich heritage and experience of the Marsh family. The firm, headed by Tom Marsh has a tradition of building excellence spanning over 18 years.

At Palmetto Builders we're proud of our reputation for building quality homes. It's this kind of experience and dedication that we bring to every home at Heartwood. That's your assurance of quality and value to enjoy for years to come.

Most recent residential home construction projects include homes in Sea Pines I and II, Palmetto Woods and The Cottages at St. Augustine Beach. In addition, the firm has several years of commercial construction experience building commercial office condominiums and renovating commercial interior space.

Whatever the scope of work, Palmetto Builders understands what it takes to build fine quality products for their customers and build great relationships along the way. As a resident of Heartwood, your home will reflectthe value, comfort and quality that comes with owning a Palmetto Builders built home.
